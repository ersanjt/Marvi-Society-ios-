---

## 3. Format playbook

### Reels (priority — highest reach)

- 15–30 sec; hook in first 2 sec
- Trending audio (licensed) + captions burned in
- Templates:
  - “POV: You find a free dinner 200m away”
  - “Agency emails vs Marvi Society”
  - “Day in the life of a Marvi creator”

### Carousels

- “How to apply” (5 slides)
- “4 collaboration types explained”
- “What venues get from Marvi”

### Stories (daily)

- Poll: “Which neighborhood next?”
- Countdown: event launches
- Repost creator tags
- Swipe-up / link sticker to app

### Collab posts

- Co-author with creators and venues on Reels
- Pooled engagement on partner grids (see Collabb/TSS pattern)

---

## 4. Launch phases

### Pre-launch (4 weeks before beta)

- Teaser Reels: blurred venue shots + “Something exclusive is coming”
- Waitlist link in bio
- DM outreach to 50 micro-influencers (5K–20K)

### Beta launch

- 3 hero Reels with real beta creators
- Venue announcement posts (tag partners)
- Story highlights: `Apply` · `How it works` · `Venues` · `FAQ`

### Growth

- Weekly creator spotlight
- Monthly venue partner reel
- UGC repost policy (with permission + @mention)

## 7. Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Show real collaborations | Fake follower counts |
| Reply to DMs within 24h | Repost without creator permission |
| Localize TR + EN captions | Only product screenshots |
| Tag venue partners | Over-promise free luxury to everyone |
| Age-appropriate content for 18+ venues | Alcohol-heavy content without age context |
